There a total of three models in FFxx group:
FF31 14 pins, 8 outputs
FF32 28 pins, 18 outputs
FF34 40 pins, 29 outputs

FF31 is completely unknown, possibly it doesn't exist at all.
FF32 is the most popular one, it has 6 pins in block 'A', and 8 ones in block 'B'
FF34 is the known only through its specs and @keptenkurk description on his blog.
It has 8 pins in 'A', 16 pins in 'B' and 5 pins in 'C'.

Consider using this data to build something more generic.
